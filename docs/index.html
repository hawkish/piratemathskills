Subscriptions & Purchases

PirateMathSkills offers optional in-app purchases managed through RevenueCat. To process and manage subscriptions, RevenueCat collects the following data on our behalf:

  • Purchase history — to verify and restore purchases
  • Device & technical information — device type, OS version, and app version
  • User identifier — an anonymous ID to associate purchases with your account

This data is used solely for subscription management and app functionality. RevenueCat does not sell your personal data. For more details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
